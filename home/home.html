Intro

Hello, and welcome to my site!

I'm a computer scientist focused on computer graphics and physics programming. I'm also an indie game/app dev, digital artist, novelist, cybersecurity and I.T. enthusiast, and embedded engineer.

I love to run (mostly 5Ks, 10Ks, half marathons, and marathons), lift weights, watch lots of animation, play video games, visit theme parks, and practice clarinet, voice, and piano.

I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science: Animation and Games Emphasis from Brigham Young University in 2019, with minors in Creative Writing and Mathematics. As part of my Creative Writing minor, I applied and was accepted into Brandon Sanderson's limited-enrollment course, Writing Science Fiction and Fantasy. I then completed my M.S. in Computer Science in 2024, focusing on computational foam and bubble simulation.
