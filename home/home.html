Alex Neville

Engineering | Music | Theme Parks | Gaming

I'm a computer scientist, indie game dev, and I.T. enthusiast. While my specialty is computer graphics and physics programming (fluid simulation in particular), I also have strong interests in cybersecurity, hardware engineering, and embedded systems!

I also enjoy coding video games, apps, and websites!

When I'm not programming, I write novels (fantasy, science fiction, and horror), make 2D and 3D digital art, run, lift weights, watch lots of animation, and play clarinet and piano. I love roller coasters, carnival rides, and water slides, and summer is my favorite season. So far, I've ridden 136 roller coasters across the United States!

My side interests include dinosaurs, geysers, psychology, Legos, lucid dreaming and OBEs, and astronomy.

I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science: Animation Emphasis from Brigham Young University, with minors in Creative Writing and Mathematics. As part of my Creative Writing minor, I applied and was accepted into Brandon Sanderson's limited-enrollment course, Writing Science Fiction and Fantasy. I then obtained my M.S. in Computer Science, focusing on computational bubble simulation. In the future, I plan to study electrical and mechanical engineering, and may aim for a Ph.D. in Computer Science as well.
